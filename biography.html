Haimiao Zhang

About me

  • I received my B.S. degree from Department of applied mathematics at Guiyang University (GYU) in 2013, Ph.D degree from Department of applied mathematics at Wuhan University (WHU) in 2018. I visited Prof. Bin Dong at BICMR of Peking University (PKU) from June, 2016 to Sep., 2017. From July, 2018 to Sep., 2020, I worked at BICMR of PKU as a postdoc researcher. From Sep, 2020 to now, I am working at Beijing Information Science and Technology University (BISTU).
